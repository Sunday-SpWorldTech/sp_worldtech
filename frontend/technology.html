Technology architecture for frontend, platform, database, services, and deployment.

Frontend

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, responsive UI, professional pages, user dashboard, and staff portal.

Platform

Node.js, Express, JWT authentication, role-based routes, and dashboard services.

Database
